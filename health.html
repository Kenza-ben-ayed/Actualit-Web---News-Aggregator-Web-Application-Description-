Tunisian Youth Lead COVID-19 Response

Oumaima Dhahri, a youth participant in USAID's Ma3an Tunisia program, decided to put her abilities to use to help her country’s response to the spread of COVID-19. Oumaima shared how the Ma3an project influenced her COVID-19 response initiative: “The work to make masks has been intense. Each time my team has faced conflict, though, I’ve been able to apply skills learned from Ma3an. I also remember advice from the Ma3an team that has helped me manage team dynamics and communicate better. Ma3an was an enriching learning experience that’s paying off for me today.” "I hope that we can all ‘shield-up’ against COVID-19. Tunisian youth are capable of so much!" – Oumaima Dhahri.

WOMEN-CARE : Programme de dépistage du cancer du col de l'utérus en unité mobile pour les femmes en Tunisie

Nous sommes ravis d'annoncer que le Centre tunisien de santé publique a reçu un financement du gouvernement australien pour lancer un programme de dépistage du cancer du col de l'utérus en unité mobile en Tunisie. Le programme sera mis en œuvre en partenariat avec l'Office national de la famille et de la population (ONFP) et l'Association tunisienne de lutte contre les maladies sexuellement transmissibles et le sida (ATL MST SIDA Tunis). Le cancer du col de l'utérus est un problème de santé publique important en Tunisie, où il est le deuxième cancer le plus fréquent chez les femmes

HEALTHCoronavirus-Review of the week of April 10 to 16,
2023: 09 deaths and 221 new contaminations

The Ministry of Health announced that 9 deaths and 221 new contaminations by the Coronavirus were recorded from April 10 to 16, 2023. The death toll stood at 29,378 and that of positive cases reached 1,152,483. 1049 analyzes were performed. The amount of analyzes carried out since the start of the pandemic is 5,009,089. The positivity rate of the tests carried out reached: 21.06%. According to the same source, the number of new patients admitted to hospitals and private clinics reached 31 until April 16, 2023.
